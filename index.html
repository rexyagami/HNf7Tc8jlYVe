About

Symphony being the trendsetter in the air-cooler market and a consumer-centric brand, with its first-ever innovation challenge, wishes to engage with young developers and innovators in India.

Since its inception, our air coolers have been an inspiration and a symbol for the futuristic and original design. This is probably why we are the most recognized and copied air cooler brand in the world. At present, the organized market is flooded with at least 25 brands (includes some smaller regional players too). If we talk of the entire universe of branded air coolers in India, the basic design and technology used are more or less the same, with just a handful of outliers, maybe. The competition is getting stiff and the market overall is heading towards saturation, in terms of innovation. In such a scenario, it becomes crucial to differentiate ourselves from the competition and become the most preferred brand when consumers think of air coolers. We are looking for some disruptive product ideas on two fronts: Design and Technology. It could be something never heard of, or could even be something very common but never thought of. The only thing to bear in mind is that it shouldn’t just be something good to have. Rather, it needs to be something that addresses some consumer tension at a reasonable cost. If the cost of features becomes too high then the purpose of being competitive will be lost.

FAQ

Who can participate in Symphony Coolathon?

The Symphony Coolathon is open to students, technologists, freelancers, and the professional developer community.

What is the cost of participating in Symphony Coolathon?

There is no participation fee.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully working?

The entry should be functional for review by the judges.

A fully developed application needs to have a solid backend for data storage like SQL, SharePoint etc. As it is an online hackathon, is it acceptable to show only the prototype?

Yes, you are required to submit a prototype. If you use any database like SQL Server or PostgreSQL, you can also submit a database dump along with your submission.

DirectX

Symphony delivers market-leading products that are a wonderful combination of design innovation, energy efficiency, distinctive styling, and customer-centricity. As a pioneer in the air-cooling industry, Symphony has continually been at the forefront of innovation. From introducing the first-ever plastic body air cooler to equipping them with world-class features viz. cool flow dispenser, i-Pure technology, touch panel, pop-up control panel, easy-fill, just to name a few, Symphony has been a trendsetter in the market.

Keeping in line with the trend of innovation and its vision to bring disrupting technology into air coolers Symphony is looking forward to having the best of design solutions that are not only too good to be true but also feasible technologically. The teams will be required to come up with prototypes in the later stage of the hackathon once their designs are approved.
